Reversi Game Rules

Objective

Have the majority of disks of your color on the board at the end of the game.

Game Setup

The game starts with 4 disks placed in the center of the board: 2 black, 2 white.

Gameplay

  • Black always moves first.
  • Players alternate turns placing one disk on an empty square.
  • A move must capture at least one opponent's disk.
  • Disks are captured by "outflanking" opponent's disks.
  • If you cannot make a valid move, your turn is skipped.
  • The game ends when neither player can make a valid move.

Winning

When the game ends, the player with the most disks of their color wins.
